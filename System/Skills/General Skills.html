There are 30 General Skills, each subdivided in Sub-Skills, that represent different facets of the skill. Whenever you put point in a General Skills, you gain a point in each Sub-Skill.
You can only raise General Skills that have all Sub-Skills at the same level.
Alternative Rule :
To raise a General Skills with different levels in its Sub-Skill, take the lowest level Sub-Skill, the cost to raise the General Skill is 3 x Lowest Level plus 2 x ( Sub-Skill Level - Lowest Level) for each Sub-Skill. This ensures that however you level up your skills/sub-skills, you always pay the same amount of xp

Each Sub-Skill is associated with its own Characteristic which corresponds to what we feel is the most logical Characteristic to use in most situations. For some dice, another characteristic might actually be more appropriate. The GM should allow some substitutions from time to time.
In some situations, multiple skills could be applicable, even if we've tried to neatly separate most skills for their respective use. In this situation I think it is best to encourage player to negotiate from time to time on whether or not a certain skill is applicable instead of the intended one (of course, these demands from the players should be done in moderation as not to overburden the GM)

Administration

Anything regarding administrative processes, the law of the different states and the official documents associated.

Legislation (Brain)

Knowledge of the law

Forgery (Dexterity)

Capacity to create false documents

Animals

Husbandry (Instincts)

The rearing and taming of wild animals.

  • Special Rule (Taming) :
  • Extended Husbandry + Instincts Dice Roll, with a difficulty of 2 x (Animal Rank + (6- Brain)) with a duration of 1 week.
    If the animal has been captured, reduce duration to 1 day.
    If the species has a family structure and you've proven yourself stronger than the beast, difficulty is (Animal Rank + (6- Brain) instead

Riding (Body)

Riding horses and other animals.

Botany

Foraging (Instincts)

Used when searching for wildlife, plants and mushrooms.

Herbalist (Wisdom)

Used when crafting potions, knowledge of the effects of diverse plants and remedies

Commerce

Appraisal (Wisdom)

Used when confirming value of goods.

Negotiation (Aura)

Used to negotiate, bargain, barter etc...

Construction

Craft (Dexterity)

General ability to work with raw materials and build simple contraptions. This would be used to build ladders, tents, rope mechanisms, simple traps. For more specialized uses and crafts, a Personal Skill should be taken.

Engineering (Brain)

Designing and understanding how different contraption works, what their purpose is and what are its weak points.

Cooking

Taste/Smell (Instincts)

Same as Awareness but specialized on recognizing smell and tastes. Useful for detecting poison or dangerous gazes

Cooking (Wisdom)

Knowing how to cook good and balanced meal, with or without proper equipment. Also includes knowledge of how to make edible food out of dangerous/potentially harmful ingredients

Debate

Philosophy (Brain)

Knowledge of the various school of thoughts in the world, ability to write interesting thesis and develop critical opinion on other work of the sort

Rhetoric (Aura)

Used when trying to convince an oratory through facts and logic

Determination

Constitution (Body)

Resistance to toxins, spoiled food, diseases

Willpower (Brain)

Resistance to seduction, fear, rousing speeches and other mental effects.

Discretion

Stealth (Body)

Move without being seen or heard

Inconspicuous (Aura)

Used when trying not to look suspicious / be detected while in a public space with other people

Espionage

Eavesdropping (Instincts)

Gathering rumors and overhearing conversations

Sleight of Hand (Dexterity)

Lock-picking, card tricks, Legerdemain, etc..

Grapple

Special Use (Combat) : See Combat#Grapple

Grapple / Strength (Body)

Used when trying to accomplish feats of pure Strength such as lifting a heavy rock or subduing a adversary

Contortionist (Dexterity)

Used when escaping bonds or holds or when trying to fight through tight spaces

Grit

Endurance (Body)

Used mostly as a defensive Skill when resisting fatigue, extreme environmental conditions.

Focus (Brain)

Used when trying to ignore distractions on a task. Also kind of vigilance against speech trickery

Hunting

Trapping (Dexterity)

Knowledge of trap making, animal habits and habitats.

Tracking (Instincts)

Ability to identify animals based on their footprints and excrement.

Impersonation

Disguise (Dexterity)

Physical Disguise

Mimicry (Aura)

Impersonation of someone's voice and mannerism.

Impressions

Charm (Aura)

Used when trying to give a positive impression to another character (seduction or just pretending to be a good guy)

Manners (Dexterity)

How to conduct oneself properly in high society.

Information

Analysis (Brain)

Logical deduction and puzzle-solving skills. Should not be used to skip puzzles setup by the GM (unless the group really isn't into the whole puzzle solving shtick but you still want the smart character to show off) but give hints about how some pieces of information might be relevant, etc...

Research (Wisdom)

Used when searching for a piece of information in a vast amount of data. Knowing where to search. Most of the time, this would be an Extended Test to get a piece of information.

Instruction

Training (Body)

Training Characters. Special Rules Coming

Teaching (Wisdom)

Teaching stuff to characters. Special Rules Coming

Investigation

Inquiry (Aura)

The subtle art of asking delicate questions without getting punched in the face.
Potential Risk (Opponents Empathy) : People realizing what you're after. Reducible by Composure

Scrutiny (Instincts)

Search a room for clues, footprints, etc... Find anomalies or detect people

Knowledge

Lore (Wisdom)

General knowledge as in accumulation of facts and information

Science (Brain)

Basis of science : math, chemistry, etc...

Language

Calligraphy (Dexterity)

How to write beautiful letters and impress uptight aristocrats

Read (Wisdom)

General knowledge of the most common languages around the world : how to read them, general history and evolution of the language.

Leadership

Command (Aura)

Field leadership and management. Used to motivate troops, restore morale, maneuver squad around the battlefield.
Advanced rules coming soon

Strategy (Brain)

Large scale strategies in the war effort. Special Rules coming soon.

Medicine

Dressing (Dexterity)

How to treat wounds : suture, dress and low level surgery.

Diagnostic (Wisdom)

Recognizing illness from symptoms and patient history and knowing how to prepare a remedy

Spectacle

Dance (Aura)

How to perform beautiful dances.

Instruments (Dexterity)

How to perform beautiful melodies. Characters are expected to choose an instrument. Whenever using another instrument, use only half of the skill value instead.

Observation

Awareness (Instincts)

Passive perception of things around you. If you're making an active effort to find something use General Skills#Scrutiny (Instincts) instead.

Empathy (Aura)

Reading social clues on the face of your interlocutor to determine trustworthiness and hidden agendas.

Orienteering

Orientation (Instincts)

Used to orient oneself without the use of tools or exterior help, relying purely on instincts and prior knowledge of the area.

Navigation (Brain)

Used to orient oneself with tools and techniques (following stars, using a compass and a map...)

Persuasion

Subtle art of making people do what you want them to do.

Intimidation (Body)

Either by force

Diplomacy (Aura)

Or by appealing to their kind nature

Physique

Athletics (Body)

Running, jumping, climbing, throwing...

Balance (Instincts)

Used when balancing on ropes or similar precarious situations

Steel

Reflexes (Instincts)

Quick reactions to unexpected events.

Composure (Brain)

Used in social encounters when trying to keep your cool, hide true intentions or maintain a poker face. Usually opposed by Empathy

Storytelling

Legends (Wisdom)

Knowledge of old folktales and stories.

Oratory (Aura)

Oral expressions when performing with the objective of getting people's attention

Way of the Land

Camouflage (Body)

Setting up ambushes and hiding betwixt the trees

Nature (Wisdom)

General knowledge of nature as well as area lore (local plants and animals, natural resources)
