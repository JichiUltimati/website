When a Character's at Death's door, they unlock a new ability, called Combat#Limit Break. Limit Breaks can only be used once per combat. Each Limit Break is associated to a music of the player's choosing.
Limit Breaks have 4 Characteristics:

  • Activation
    How the Limit Break is activated : as soon as the character reaches Death's Door, on his turn, for free, for an action.
    When a Limit Break is activated by a player, immediately start playing the associated song.

  • Immediate Effect
    What happens when the Limit Break is activated.

  • Lasting Effect
    As long as the Limit Break is in effect, these passive effect apply

  • Duration
    Real time duration of the Limit Break. Yes real time duration.

Limit Break List

You gonna go far kid

Side Action. Choose an allied character.
Lasting. This Character gains an additional main action each turn

Fucked with an anchor

Main Action. Damage and Root any target.
Lasting : Root remains

The Pheonix

Main Action. Restore all allies to full life.
Lasting : If this ends naturally, they die. Horribly.

All Eyes on You

Auto. Skip to the start of your turn.
Lasting : No other Limit Break can be activated

Boom Bitch, get out the way

Lasting : Replace all defaults with True Eject

The Kiss of Death

Main Action. Kill a non-boss enemy in melee range.

Bring the Hammer Down

Side Action. Lasting : All Dice Rolls are Critical Rolls.

Smoke Weed Everyday.

Lasting. All Characters are Disoriented.

Those who Gather Stars in the Dark

Main Action. Fuse with all character at Death's Door in the Ultimate Fighting Entity.
Combat Characteristics are Best of all +1 for each character in the Mechazord. Acts at each of participants initiative.

My name is Legion

Free Action. Lasting. ALL Summons copy the actions of their Master. You may chose new target for the copies.

Avenged Sevenfold

Automatically. You perform a Main Action. If you target the character that made you reach Death's Door, ignore all restriction on range for the purpose of targeting.

A Will Carried On

Repeat previous Limit Break that is currently playing.

To Hell and Back

Side Action. All Allies can reach Death's Door as a Free Action.
Lasting : Activating a Limit Break becomes a Free Action

Brilliant Wings

Lasting : Gain Flight. Movement speed is doubled while flying.

The Attack of the Dead Men

Main Action. All Allies that are unconscious immediately rise up at Death's Door until end of the song.

1812 Ouverture

Free Action. Reload. Lasting : Reload is a Free Action

Invincible

Auto. Lasting. You cannot die
