General

Adaptable - 2

You can make do with almost anything.
You do not require any kind of specialized tools for a skill check (as long as these tools are reasonably small)

Adaptable (Improved) - 3

Furthermore, you do not require any specialized facilities for crafting

Affinity with (Skill) - 1

Gain a +1 in a Skill of your choosing. Cannot be taken more than once for the same skill.

Animal Companion - 2

Start the game with a critter (chosen in Bestiary, low level) that follows you around and that can understand simple orders. This animal cannot fight for you as a Soul Forging#Summon (Beast) unless you pick up the restriction and choose it to be your summon. You can spend Fate Points to save this Companion.

Animal Companion (Improved) - 4

Expend the list of critters you can choose from. Also, at this level you and the critter can somewhat understand each other.

Blessed by the Gods - 2

You start the game with an additional Fate Point. You can take this trait multiple times

Common Sense - 1

"Common sense isn't that common". Whenever you would make a decision that is unbelievably dumb, the GM will warn you of the nature of this action and of its foreseeable consequences.

Death Prophecy - 3

You have received a prophecy of your death. This prophecy is vague and uncertain but you are convinced that your death will follow the prophecy. When picking this trait, settle on a death prophecy with your GM. It should be vague (e.g. Ars Magicka).

Disease Immunity - 3

You are immune to disease and most common toxins

Fearless - 2

You fear nothing and no one. This cannot be taken with Negative Traits that would make you afraid of something.

Deep Pockets - 1

Once per game session, you can find a small object in your pocket. The object must fit in a pocket or small bag, must be inexpensive and its presence in your pocket must somewhat makes sense (i.e. you could pull a wrench or a sandwich but not a diamond)

Foresight - 2

You never forget to pack anything, and always have common necessary equipment with you (e.g. ropes and torches for exploring a cave, sunscreen when going to the beach...). This doesn't apply to special equipment such has tools for a trade you don't practice.

Lesser Spiritualism - 1

You can sense the presence of ghost and the accumulation of negative energy. You can also converse with these spirits if they are intellient enough to do so.

Nature's Calling - 1

No Penalties when Mimicking Animals

Precise - 1

You can choose to deal non-lethal damage whenever you attack someone. If you kill, it is by choice

Relic

You start the game with a powerful artifact. Upcoming Rules

Speed Reading - 1

In just a few minutes you can read through a whole book and single pages in almost an instant.

Total Recall - 1

You never forget anything, even the smallest details.

Totem Animal - 2

You may purchase Traits corresponding to your totem animal at their listed cost.

Way of the (Land) - 1

In the specific terrain, ignore all Difficult Terrains, and gain a +1 to any survival/foraging/knowledge Tests. (e.g. Desert, Cities, Forests, Mountains, Swamps...)

Wealthy - 1

You come from a rich family, or just recently struck gold. Whatever the source, you are wealthy enough to not have to worry about money for the foreseeable future.
Multiply Starting money by 10.

Wealthy (Improved) - 3

You come from a rich family, or just recently struck gold. Whatever the source, you are wealthy enough to not have to worry about money for the foreseeable future.
Multiply Starting money by 100.

Craft

Speed Worker - 1

Whenever you make an Extended Test, divide time spent by Half

Social

Chaste - 1

You are immune to seduction of any form

Contacts(Organisation, City...) - 1

Choose one organisation, city or ... These people know you, and you will usually be able to get in touch with people related to the place.

Eye Candy / Androgynous - 1

With your looks and charisma, you could seduce just about anybody.
As long as your target has attraction towards a specific demographic, you can attempt to seduce it

First Impressions - 1

+1 to Social Skill Checks with people you've just met

First Impressions (Improved) - 2

Guaranteed Critical on your first roll when interacting with someone you've met for the first time

Guild Member - 2

You are a member of a craftsman guild. As such you can use their facilities without paying. They can also serve as contacts to get a job or information.

Combat

Alert - 3

You can never be taken by Surprise. You are always allowed a Defense against an attack targeting you and act as normal during Surprise Rounds

Extraordinary Stamina - 2

Increase Max Strain by 1

Glass Cannon - 2

Increase Damage by 1, reduce any Armor worn by 1

Iron Skull - 2

You cannot be Stunned or Knocked Out.

Leap Up - 1

Standing back Up is a Free Action

Lucky - 1

If possible, you are never the target of random effects.

No Sacrifice, No Victory - 2

Whenever you would gain Strain, you can instead cross the next Threshold.

Paranoia - 1

Increase Initiative by 1.

Quick Draw - 1

You can ready any number of weapons as a Free Action.

Soul Slot - 2

Gain a Soul Forging Slot. Can be taken up to 2 times.

Animal-Restricted

Amphibious - 2

You can breathe underwater

Bio-luminescence - 1

You glow in the Dark. You can see in Short Range even in the Dark. But anyone can see you. This can be turned off.

Burning Blood - 2

When you cross a Threshold apply Ablaze to all characters in Melee.

Camouflage - 2

While standing still and naked, you are almost invisible to the human eye. Automatically pass any Stealth Check as long a you possess the skill.

Cat fall - 2

Ignore Fall Damage

Climate Adaption - 1

You don't need to make Survival Checks in the selected climate

Cold Blooded - 1 / 1

Immune to Traits#Thermal Sight.
Cannot survive in cold places. Automatically fail any Survival/Endurance check in cold climates.

Corrosive Spit - 1

You can chew your way through most metallic objects. Takes around 10 minutes to gnaw through a standard metal bar.

Echolocation - 1

In confined spaces, can be used to detect obstacles

Exceptional (Characteristic) - 2

Your maximum in 1 characteristic is raised by 1. (Only the maximum, and not your actual)

Extensible Tongue - 1

Your tongue extends like a frog's. It is sticky and can allow you to grab small objects at a distance

Fins - 1

Swimming is not considered as Difficult Terrain.

Flight - 5

Rudimentary Flight. Low Altitude, Must Move Each Turn. Double Movement Speed. Strain to Perform Other Actions.

Flight (Improved) - 8

Choose One. Capable of stationary flight OR flying for long periods of time OR flying with Equipment. You can take this bonus again to choose another option.

Glide - 2

You have a slow, primitive and limited form of Flight that allows you to glide down from heights in total safety. You can ascend with favorable wind currents.

Natural Armour - 1

You always count as having at least 2 points of Armor

Natural Armour (Improved) - 3

You always count as having at least 4 points of Armor

Natural Climber - 1

Can climb pretty much anything.

Night Vision - 2

Can see just as well in the darkness as in broad daylight.

Migratory Instincts - 1

You always know where the North is. With a bit of time you will always make it back home.

Pack Leader - 3

Animals from the same species obey you without having to make Handling Checks. Furthermore Soul Forging#Summon (Beast) from your species don't cost Soul Forging Points

Keen Sense of Smell - 2

Gain +1 to Tracking. You can recognize people and objects by their smell. You can detect enemies in Short Range of you, even in total Darkness.

Regeneration - 1

Your body heals wounds naturally at a slow pace. It takes a single week to fully heal any injuries.

Regeneration (Improved) - 3

Same as before, but only takes a single day.

Thermal Sight - 1

Can see most living beings even in total darkness

Toxic - 3

You can produce a slow acting poison. Once ingested by someone or injected in their bloodstream, it takes about a few days to take effect and gradually incapacitates the victim. Without proper treatment, it is fatal

Water Running - 1

You can traverse (but not stand on) bodies of water during your Move Actions.

Banes

General

Blind - 3

You are blind. You can longer target characters with anything (Targeting ALL and randomly is still possible). For any other test requiring eyesight, apply a -6 Penalty

Coward - 1

You never go first. People will need to attach and throw you to get you to check something first.

Deaf - 2

You are deaf. Any Skill Check based on audition automatically fails. You cannot defend against attack that are not in your field of view.

Curse of Venus - 1

All the wrong people fall in love with you. Your romantic relationships never go right. Whenever you want or need to seduce someone, it goes horribly wrong.

Curse of Pacifism - 3

You cannot target a character that has not attacked you first.

Curse of Truth - 2

You can't lie, even by omission. You are compelled to tell the truth in any situation

Curse of Silence - 2

Whenever you speak/write a word, gain 1 Strain until the end of the day.

Curse of Vocabulary - 2

Choose 2 x Wisdom Words. You cannot say/write/express any other word

Curse of Vocabulary (Improved) - 3

Choose Wisdom Words instead.

Curse of Lies - 1

You can never state the truth.

Curse of Lies (Improved) - 2

Whenever you lie, you must exaggerate your lies even further

Curse of Poverty - 3

You can never own anything. Any possession or money you keep with you tends to mysteriously disappear. You're lucky to wake up with your clothes each morning

Curse of the Fae - 1

You can never enter a building without being invited first by the owner or by a proxy.

Curse of Duality - 2

Whatever you do, you do it twice. Just to be sure. Whether it is attacking an enemy or climbing a wall.

Curse of Nudity

You can no longer wear Armor of any kind.

Fatigued - 1

You only recover your Strain after a good night's rest

Incompetent (Characteristic) - 1

You can not level up Skills associated with the chosen Characteristic.

Light Sleeper - 1

Whenever you sleep outside, gain 2 Strain for the Day

Loneliness - 2

If you have no allies in Long Range, you will curl up into a ball and cry.

Motion Sickness - 1

Whenever you use a vehicle for transportation, max out your Strain for the Rest of the Day

Night Owl - 1

All Dice Rolls are at -1 during the day.

No Sense of Direction - 1

You tend to get lost often. Even with the right tools you just don't seem to be able to go where you want. Should you ever need to roll a die to find your way, consider it a Critical Failure.

Obsessed with - 1

You are obsessed with a very specific, but weird detail about people's lives. Whenever you meet someone, you have to ask them about this specific detail. You are persistent and will only stop when people start shouting at you.

Obsessed with (Improved) - 2

Same thing but the only way you'll stop asking is if you're beaten black and blue by someone. Even then you might try again in the future, or pay someone to get the information for you.

Orderly - 2

"Order is the barrier that holds back the flood of death. We must all of us on this train of life remain in our allotted station. We must each of us occupy our preordained particular position"
Whatever you do, even in combat, you do it respecting a predefined order : you always hit people from smallest to tallest, you always choose the right-most path first.

Poor - 1

You start the game with no gear, and no money

Prideful - 1

"La dÃ©pendance est une faiblesse". You will never ask anyone for help, even if it will cost you your life. If someone still helps you after you refused, you will do everything to prove you didn't need it etc....

Self-Doubt - 1

Whenever you fail a Skill Check or Combat Check, gain 1 Strain

Stressed - 1

You can no longer take risks on Skill Checks and Combat Rolls

Unlucky - 1

Whenever something bad happens, it happens to you. Whenever a target is chosen at random, it chooses you.

Weak (Characteristic) - 1

Reduce chosen Characteristic (and associated Maximum) by 1

Combat

Anxious - 1

Gain 1 Strain each time you roll a Dice in Combat.

Anxious (Improved) - 2

Gain 1 Stress for the Day whenever you have to perform a Dice Roll

Apathy - 1

You don't have a Limit Break.

Apathy (Improved) - 3

When you reach Death's Door, die instead.

Downward Spiral - 1

Whenever you gain Strain, gain 1 more

Drained - 1

Take 1 Strain if you move more than a Short Range in a single Turn.

Failure - 1

You can no longer Critical Strike (Result of the dice roll is what you would have added)

Failure (Improved) - 2

Dice result of Critical Strike is considered Critical Failure.

Fear of Death - 1

Gain 1 Strain each time you cross a Threshold

Forgotten by Fate - 2

You no longer Roll Dice in Combat. All Dice result count as 0 instead.

Laziness - 1

You can no longer Strain to gain a Side Action

Numb - 1

You no longer gain the bonuses from HP Thresholds

Self-Harm - 1

Start each combat at 2nd HP Threshold.

Weak (Combat Characteristic) - 1

-1 to chosen Characteristic

Weak (Combat Characteristic) (Improved) - 3

Chosen Characteristic becomes 0

Animal-Restricted

Bio-luminescence - 1

You glow in the Dark. You can see in Short Range even in the Dark. But anyone can see you. This cannot be turned off.

Critter Spook - 1

Animals hate you. You are targeted first by any wild animals you fight, and you get a -6 to tame animals.
